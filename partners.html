Models & tiers

How collaboration works

We keep the framework simple, while allowing enough flexibility to fit your operational and financial model.

At the core of Kemet Gift lies a tiered structure – from E1 to L1 – allowing you to serve different visitor segments without diluting your brand. You can deploy the full ladder or focus on a subset that fits your audience.

• E1–C1: accessible price points, optimized for volume and entry tickets.
• P1–S1: elevated gifts, ideal for core retail and special bundles.
• G1–L1: premium and numbered pieces for VIP programs and limited drops.

Financially, you can opt for classic wholesale, shared-margin retail, or fully co-branded editions with agreed volumes and exclusivity windows.

Example structures

• National museum: full ladder E1–L1, mixing high-volume visitors with curated collector releases.
• Boutique hotel: focus on P1–S1 as lobby retail and VIP gifting, with a co-branded note inside the box.
• Cultural district: rotating G1/L1 editions tied to events, festivals, or seasonal campaigns.

All configurations sit on top of documented governance and quality standards, so the experience stays consistent across venues and phases of growth.
