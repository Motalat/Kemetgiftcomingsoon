The Philosophy: "The Weight of History"

At Kemet Gift, we believe history shouldn't just be read; it should be held. We don't make souvenirs that fade; we forge connections that last.

1. Material Integrity (Solid Brass)

We rejected common alloys like zinc and aluminum. History is heavy, and so are our pieces. Each medal is crafted from solid, high-density brass (C360) or bronze, providing a substantial weight (~37.5g) and a authentic metallic resonance that cheap replicas cannot mimic.

2. The Minting Process (Forged, Not Poured)

Our pieces are not cast in molds; they are struck with immense hydraulic pressure. Using hardened steel dies and double or triple striking techniques, we achieve museum-grade relief depth and razor-sharp details that honor the complexity of ancient Egyptian art.

3. The Ritual of Unboxing

The box is not just a wrapper; it is the first chapter of the story. From rigid kraft boxes to premium soft-touch casing, the packaging is engineered to build anticipation and respect for the artifact inside.
