Frequently Asked Questions

What are the medals made of?

All Kemet Gift medals are forged from solid, high-density brass or bronze. We do not use cheap base metals like zinc or aluminum.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide via secure courier partners. Rates are calculated at checkout.

How do I care for my medal?

Your piece is protected by an anti-tarnish layer. Wipe gently with a soft, dry microfiber cloth.
